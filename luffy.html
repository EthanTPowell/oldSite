Monkey D. "Strawhat" Luffy

Captain of the Strawhat Pirates from East Blue

Devil's fruit: Rubber fruit

Monkey D. Luffy is the rising star of the pirate world. He took to the seas three years ago to become the pirate king. He has gathered a crew of ten people who are all highly specialized in their fields of expertise. He has become the hope of many by taking territory from other pirate crews and challenging powerful pirates all through the grand line. Luffy at one point ate the rubber fruit and turned into a rubber human with the ability to stretch his body in all sorts of creative ways which he uses to come up with many varied and creative attacks. He also uses his rubber body to increase his blood flow and multiply his speed as well as inflate his bones to massively increase his destructive power. He also has mastered armament, observation, and conqueror's haki.
