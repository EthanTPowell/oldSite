Kaidou "The World's Strongest Creature"

Captain of the Beast Pirates from the Grand Line

Devil's fruit: The Mythical Zoan Fish: Model Azure Dragon

While Whitebeard was known as the strongest man alive, Kaidou is feared all around the world as the Strongest Creature alive. His strength is inhuman which is fitting as he leads the infamous crew that is the beaast pirates. His crew is chock full of zoan devil fruit users. This is because he arranged for Caesar Clown and Doflamingo to create artificial zoan devil fruits for his crew. His fruit transforms him into an Azure Dragon that can breathe fire and create fire clouds that he can use to fly or float entire islands miles in the air. Kaidou is also a master of all forms of haki, just like the rest of the emperors. His primary form of attack in his humanoid form is with his thunder Bagua which is essentially a massive spiked metal club that he imbues with immense amounts of armament haki. He sailed with the Rocks Pirates alongside Charlotte Linlin for years before founding the Beast Pirates.
