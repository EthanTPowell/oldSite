Marshall D. "Blackbeard" Teach

Captain of the Blackbeard Pirates from the Grand Line

Devil's fruit: Dark Dark fruit

Marshall D. Teach is shrouded in mystery. He appeared one day and joined the Whitebeard Pirates for years until they encountered the dark dark fruit. This fruit turns the user into pure darkness and also has other abilities that are unknown to all but a select few. At that point, he killed the person who found it and defected with the fruit. he was pursued by Fire Fist Ace, but the power of Teach's fruit overwhelmed Ace and he captured Ace instead. At which point, he turned Ace in to the World Government in order to garner favor and join the ranks of the seven warlords of the sea, the select group of pirates with immunity backed by the government. His real goal was to gain their trust in order to free criminals from the prison Impel Down to form his crew while Whitebeard attacked the world government in order to rescue Ace. When whitebeard attacked, Blackbeard waited until he was weakened and somehow used the dark fruit to steal the powers of Whitebeard's Tremor fruit, said to be one of the most powerful fruits in the world, from him. After the battle, Blackbeard was declared as an emperor of the sea in Whitebeard's place and is now feared by all.
