War at Marineford

Earlier this week, the whitebeard pirates invaded the marine HQ in an effort to free their captured member Portgas D. Ace. Ace, being the head of one of Whitebeard's divisions, was sentenced to execution. That was when the pirates invaded. It became an all out war between all the military might of the world government and the might of Whitebeards's fleet. At the same time, Strawhat Luffy and Marshall D. Teach had separately staged prison breaks in Impel Down, the highest security prison in the world. Straw Hat then arrived at Marineford with the intent to help save Ace, but Teach showed up with a group of escaped convicts from Impel Down and interrupted the fight, killing whitebeard and taking the power of the tremor tremor fruit from him. At the same time, Luffy was attempting to escape with Ace when the ever-reliable Admiral Akainu carried out the execution himself and seemingly mortally wounded Straw Hat, but Red Hair shanks appeared and declared that the fighting would either stop there or he would involve himself. The world government, having already accomplished the gargantuan task of defeating an emperor, agreed to end the fighting there.
Just days later, Straw Hat Luffy infiltrated Marineford by himself to ring the Ox bell which, given its normal usage of ushering in the new year, seemed to be Straw Hat declaring the beginning of a new era.
