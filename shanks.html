Red-Hair Shanks AKA Red Hair


The Captain of the infamous Red Hair Pirates


Devil's fruit: He has none

Shanks is originally from the West Blue. He traveled with Gold Roger and his crew for years as an apprentice during Roger's travels when he was young. After Roger's execution, Shanks formed his own pirate crew and set out on the seas. He is now one of the four emperors who rule the new world, and is the one second most shrouded in mystery. Red hair has no devil's fruit abilities. He instead uses his immense haki and strength to take down his opponents. Compared to the rest of the emperors, he may at first look like a normal person, but this man could clash evenly with Whitebeard, who was once deemed the strongest man in the world. The Red Hair pirates also have complete control over the sake trade in and out of East Blue. It is reported that Red Hair passed the Straw hat that Monkey D. Luffy wears on to him after Red Hair had originally gotten it from the Pirate King, Gold Roger.
